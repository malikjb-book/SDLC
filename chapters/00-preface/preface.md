## Who Is This Book For?

This book is written for:

- **Software developers** looking to supercharge their workflow with AI tools
- **Engineering leaders** evaluating AI adoption across their PDLC
- **DevOps and platform engineers** exploring intelligent automation
- **Process architects** modernizing their development methodologies
- **Product leaders** bridging discovery, specification, and engineering execution
- **CTOs and VPs of Engineering** building AI-powered engineering organizations

## How to Read This Book

The book is organized in six parts:

1. **Part I — Foundations (Chapters 0–4):** What the product lifecycle is, how it has evolved, and the AI landscape
2. **Part II — Discover & Specify (Chapters 5–6):** From market insight through executable specification
3. **Part III — Build & Deliver (Chapters 7–12):** The engineering lifecycle, powered by AI agents at every phase
4. **Part IV — Operate & Evolve (Chapters 13–14):** AI agents as product components, operational intelligence
5. **Part V — Platform, Governance & Economics (Chapters 15–20):** Cross-cutting infrastructure, documentation, security, cost management
6. **Part VI — Modernise & Scale (Chapters 21–22):** Legacy transformation and real-world adoption playbook

You can read sequentially for a complete journey, or jump directly to the PDLC phase most relevant to your role.
