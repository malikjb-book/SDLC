## Who Is This Book For?

This book is written for:

- **Software developers** looking to supercharge their workflow with AI tools
- **Engineering leaders** evaluating AI adoption across their SDLC
- **DevOps and platform engineers** exploring intelligent automation
- **Process architects** modernizing their development methodologies
- **CTOs and VPs of Engineering** building AI-augmented engineering organizations

## How to Read This Book

The book is organized in three parts:

1. **Foundations (Chapters 1–4):** SDLC fundamentals and the AI landscape
2. **AI Across the SDLC (Chapters 5–12):** Phase-by-phase guide to AI adoption
3. **Strategy & Scale (Chapters 13–15):** Governance, measurement, and real-world adoption

You can read sequentially for a complete journey, or jump directly to the SDLC phase most relevant to your role.

## Conventions Used

- 💡 **Tip** — Practical advice from real-world experience
- ⚠️ **Caution** — Common pitfalls to avoid
- 📋 **Prompt** — Ready-to-use prompts for AI tools
- 💻 **Code** — Runnable code examples (found in `code-samples/`)
